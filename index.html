September 28, 2024 by Samantha Brown

Unexpectedly, web development is slowly becoming a source of enjoyment for me. Among all my courses this semester, INFO2180 stands out as the most engaging. It's in this class that I truly feel like I'm stepping into the role of a programmer. What I appreciate most about web development is its surprising accessibility. It allows me to express my creativity while simultaneously acquiring new skills, making the learning process genuinely enjoyable.

There's something compelling about understanding how complex systems are designed and built that continually draws me in. Interestingly, web development and software engineering are more closely related than one might expect. Learning to create websites has improved my grasp of larger-scale software architectures, making me more aware of design patterns and system organization from the start
